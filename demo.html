Welcome to Lotto!

Rules are as follows:

Users can enter three numbers (ranging from 1 to 10) on the web page.

Each time the game starts, three winning numbers (ranging from 1 to 10 without repetition) will be randomly generated.

When the user enters three numbers and presses the draw button, the input numbers will be compared with the winning numbers (regardless of their order).

If all three numbers match, it's the first prize. If two numbers match, it's the second prize. If one number matches, it's the third prize.
